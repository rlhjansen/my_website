Intro

Me and Yannick Vinkesteijn are running a project concerning Very Large scale integration.

Simplified, this comes down to the physical architecture of hardware computerchips, which are in ever need of improvement.

Our VLSI model is a simplified version of computerchips consisting of a number of transistors, also known as logic-gates, located on the chip and a netlist containing all connections needed between the gates (the copper wiring).

goal

In our model the chip itself consists of bottom/basic layer, on which the gates are placed. Multiple additional 2-dimensional grid-like layers are available for wiring. Routing can only be done over the x, y and z axis (hence the grid-like structure). To avoid short circuits it is mandatory for the nets (copper wires) not to cross.

The goal is to connect as many nets as possible and minimize the distance to increase the communication speed between the gates and minimize heat generation.
